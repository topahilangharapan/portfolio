\textbf{Re: Backend Engineer — Tech Full-time}

\vspace{4pt}

The combination that excites me most in backend engineering is building systems that are simultaneously invisible to users yet critical to their financial security — precisely what Bibit's platform demands when it processes investment transactions for millions of retail investors. At Traveloka, engineering a \textbf{secure Credential Management System} using \textbf{AES-GCM encryption} with automated rotation and granular audit logging gave me a concrete taste of that same responsibility: protecting sensitive data at scale, where a single gap carries real operational risk.

That credential system is the clearest example of the security and data protection practices Bibit values. Beyond encryption, I built scalable backend services that processed \textbf{1,000+ daily flight-booking transactions at 99.9\% accuracy}, reducing manual intervention by \textbf{85\%} through fault-tolerant API design with configurable failover mechanisms — all in Java and Spring Boot on Kubernetes. Alongside that, I designed RESTful APIs at PT Magna Solusi Indonesia handling \textbf{10,000+ daily requests at sub-200 ms latency}, applying query optimisation on PostgreSQL to cut average response time by \textbf{35\%}. These experiences sit at the exact intersection of performance, scalability, and security that Bibit's JD outlines.

What draws me specifically to Bibit is the scale of the problem: delivering secure, performant APIs to millions of Indonesians building their first investment portfolios. I have been studying Go as my next backend language — its concurrency model and compile-time safety map naturally onto the high-throughput, low-latency services a robo-advisor demands — and Bibit's engineering culture of deep ownership over backend systems is the environment where I want to sharpen that proficiency.

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my backend experience in security-critical systems, scalable API design, and CI/CD quality practices translate to the Backend Engineer role at Bibit. I am available for a technical interview at your earliest convenience.
